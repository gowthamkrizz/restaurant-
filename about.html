About Stackly

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Stackly began with a simple idea: to serve incredible food in a welcoming space. What started as a small family diner has grown into one of New York City's most celebrated culinary destinations.

Our founders believed great food could bring people together — that philosophy is woven into everything we do, from the recipes we craft to the way we train every member of our staff.
